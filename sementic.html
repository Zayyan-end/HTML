Facts about dogs

Dogs have a highly sensitive sense of smell, are able to learn over 100 words, and can see some colors, though their perception differs from humans

A dog's sense of smell is estimated to be 10,000 to 100,000 times more sensitive than a human's, and their nose prints are unique to each individual. They can even use this ability to detect diseases.
